Given two integer arrays A and B, return the maximum length of a subarray that appears in both arrays. The subarray can appear in any order within the arrays.

Example 1:

Input:
A: [1,2,3,2,1]
B: [3,2,1,4,7]
Output: 3
Explanation: 
The repeated subarray with maximum length is [3, 2, 1].

Example 2:

Input:
A: [1,2,3,2,1]
B: [7,4,1,2,3]
Output: 3
Explanation: 
The repeated subarray with maximum length is [1, 2, 3].
